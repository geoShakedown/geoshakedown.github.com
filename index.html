About GeoShakedown

Sometimes latitude and longitude value is not enough. Why limit your the dimensions of your spatial data? Sometimes your Data Scientists need more contextual information surrounding a data point.

GeoShakedown is a service that provides geoprocessing services without a user requiring any GIS programming skills or desktop software (and costly licensing fees). Quite simply, GeoShakedown is a PostGIS-enabled geoprocessing service for organizations that need fast GIS analysis.

Need a secure connection and output without any eyes on the data from anyone not on the IRB or outside your organization, GeoShakedown can build an encrypted data processing workflow using HIPAA and IRB compliant protocols for data in transit and at rest, making it possible for an organization to have GIS capabilities without having to hire a GIS staff.

GeoShakedown is a team of highly commited geospatial data scientists, programmers and professsionals ranging from backgrounds in engineering, epidemiology and academia. GeoShakedown was built first and foremost as a tool to faciliate workflows and needs that were often time-consuming and limited to project-specific programming and software licensing. Situated on AWS RDS PostgreSQL, GeoShakedown's PostGIS processing is fast and reliable.

Do not enter personally identifiable information (PII), including latitude, longitude pairs, of any research subjects in any studies.
